Based on Fantasy Fudge skills with modifications. Governing attribute shown in parentheses.

⊕ Athletic
Jumping, Riding, Running, Throwing, Swimming, Lifting, Pushing — all governed by Strength.
⊕ Finesse
Acrobatics (Dexterity), Pick Pocketing (Reflexes), Climbing (Dexterity), Balance (Reflexes), Stealth (Pres), Sleight of Hands (Dexterity).
⊕ Combat
Read Opponent (Wisdom), Tactics (Ana), Dodging (Dexterity), Striking (Strength), Blocking (Reflexes), Aiming (Ana).
⊕ Weapon
Short Blades (Reflexes), Long Blades (Strength), Poles (Reflexes), Bows (Reflexes), Blunt & Hacking (Strength).
⊕ Natural Sciences
Mathematics, Science, Engineering, Baking/Cooking (Ana) · Health, Botany, Cartography (Mem) · Lockpicking, Orienteering (Wisdom).
⊕ Social Sciences
Linguistic, Philosophy, Law, Tracking (Wisdom) · History, Lore (Mem) · Theology, Politics (Emp).
⊕ Artistic
Poetry, Painting/Drawing, Sculpting, Music (Emp) · Handwriting (Dexterity) · Acting, Makeup, Dancing, Storytelling (Pres).
⊕ Social
Bluff, Diplomacy, Haggle, Persuasion (Emp) · Lie (Ana) · Etiquette (Mem) · Intimidation, Seduction (Pres).
